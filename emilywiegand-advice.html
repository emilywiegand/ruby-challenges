Hi my name is Emily and I have some advice for you!

Advice for when you're hitting your head against a brick wall...

Stuck? Been totally stuck for an hours? Google won't help?


TAKE A BREAK!


Go for a walk. Work on something else. Grab a coffee with a friend. Just come back to it later! A little distance from the problem always helps. You can approach it later with a fresh perspective.
